# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 8, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how **Priyansh Kashyap** ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information in our mobile games. Our games are designed for a general audience, including users ages 7 and older.

We are committed to protecting the privacy of all our players and comply with:

- Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)  
- Google Play Families Policy  
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)  

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We strive to minimize data collection to ensure a safe and streamlined gaming experience.

### Automatically Collected Data
When you play our games, we may automatically collect certain non-personal technical information, such as:

- Device type  
- Operating system version  
- General country-level location  

### Persistent Identifiers
We may collect anonymized persistent identifiers (such as the Google Advertising ID). These are used strictly for internal operations, including:

- Game analytics (if enabled in the future)  
- Ad frequency capping  
- Security and fraud prevention  

### Information You Provide
- We do **not** require registration or personal identifiers (such as names or phone numbers) to play.
- If you contact our support team, we use your email address solely to respond to and resolve your request.

---

## 2. Third-Party Services

We use industry-standard third-party services to support game functionality and stability:

### Google AdMob
- Used to display advertisements.
- All ad requests are marked as child-directed.
- Only contextual (non-personalized) ads are served.
- Behavioral or interest-based targeting is disabled.

### Firebase Firestore
We store limited profile-related information:

- Username  
- Country  
- Gender  

This data is used solely to:
- Support gameplay features  
- Enable leaderboard functionality  
- Support potential future profile display features  

We do **not** collect sensitive personal information.  
Stored data is **not** used for advertising, tracking, or profiling.

### Firebase Analytics
- Currently **not active**.
- If introduced in the future, it will only be enabled after obtaining explicit user consent.
- Any analytics collection will comply with children's privacy regulations and platform policies.
- It will only be used to improve gameplay experience.

### Firebase Crashlytics
- Used exclusively to monitor crashes and technical issues.
- Data is used strictly for diagnostics.
- Not used for analytics, advertising, or profiling.

## 3. Children’s Privacy

Our games are rated for ages 7 and above.

Because we do not implement age verification, we apply a high privacy standard to all users:

- We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
- We use only SDKs compliant with the Google Play Families Policy.
- All advertisements are contextual and age-appropriate.

---

## 4. How We Use Your Information

We process information to:

- Maintain and improve game performance  
- Monitor crashes and technical issues  
- Serve contextual, non-personalized ads  
- Protect against fraud or illegal activity  

---

## 5. Data Retention & Security

We retain information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy.

We implement reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect data. However, no method of electronic storage is completely secure.

## 6. Your Rights

Players and their parents/guardians have the following rights:

### Opt-Out of Advertising ID
You may reset your device’s Advertising ID or modify ad preferences in your Android device settings under:

`Settings → Privacy → Ads`  
or  
`Settings → Google → Ads`

### Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your data by:

- Using the in-app deletion feature:  
  `Profile → Account → Delete Account`
- Contacting us directly at:  
  **priyanshkrkashyap@gmail.com**
